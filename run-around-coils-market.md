<p><p>Run-around coils are key components in HVAC systems, facilitating heat exchange between air and water. The tubed type features tubes through which the heat transfer fluid circulates, providing efficient cooling or heating without significant pressure drop. In contrast, the finned type incorporates fins to enhance surface area, improving heat transfer efficiency. Both types cater to diverse applications, with the tubed type often suited for lower flow rates and the finned type ideal for high-efficiency requirements, driving market demand across residential and industrial sectors.</p></p>
