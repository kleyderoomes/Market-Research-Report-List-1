<p><strong>The Multilayer Inductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount Technology</li><li>Through Hole Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multilayer inductor market comprises two primary types: Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Through Hole Technology (THT). SMT inductors are designed for compact, high-density circuit boards, providing ease of assembly and reduced manufacturing costs. They are favored in modern electronics for their space-saving attributes. Conversely, THT inductors are installed through drilled holes, offering robust connections and better thermal performance, making them suitable for applications requiring durability and higher power ratings. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
