<p><p>The Semiconductor and Electronics X-Ray Inspection System market encompasses two primary types: 2D X-ray Inspection and 3D X-ray Inspection. </p><p>2D X-ray Inspection provides a flat, two-dimensional view of components, allowing for efficient detection of surface defects and internal flaws. In contrast, 3D X-ray Inspection generates a volumetric image, offering a comprehensive analysis of complex structures such as multi-layered assemblies and densely packed components. This enhanced visualization aids in more accurate diagnostics, improving quality assurance in semiconductor and electronics manufacturing processes.</p></p>
