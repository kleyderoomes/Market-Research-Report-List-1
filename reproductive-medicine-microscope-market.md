<p><p>The reproductive medicine microscope market primarily includes two types: inverted microscopes and upright microscopes. Inverted microscopes are designed for viewing samples from below, making them ideal for observing living cells in culture dishes, commonly used in infertility treatments. Upright microscopes, on the other hand, allow for imaging specimens from above and are typically utilized for tissue samples and histology. Both types play crucial roles in reproductive medicine, enabling precise analysis and enhancing the success of assisted reproductive techniques.</p></p>
