<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial X-ray cameras market primarily includes two key types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras are known for their high image quality, sensitivity, and low noise, making them ideal for detailed imaging in applications like non-destructive testing. Conversely, CMOS cameras offer advantages in speed, lower power consumption, and integrated features, which enhance their versatility in various industrial settings. Both types cater to the growing demand for precision in industrial inspection and quality control processes.</p></p>
