<p><p>The liquid fertilizer market encompasses several key types, including potash, micronutrients, phosphorous, and nitrogen. Potash provides essential potassium for plant growth, enhancing overall health and yield. Micronutrients supply trace elements like iron and zinc, vital for various physiological functions in plants. Phosphorous is crucial for energy transfer and root development, while nitrogen is essential for protein synthesis and vegetative growth.
