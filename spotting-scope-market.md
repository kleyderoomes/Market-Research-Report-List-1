<p><p>The spotting scope market is primarily divided into two types: straight body and angled body scopes. Straight body spotting scopes feature a linear design, making them ideal for quick target acquisition and easy viewing in a horizontal position. They are commonly used for birdwatching and hunting. In contrast, angled body spotting scopes offer a diagonal view, providing a more comfortable viewing angle, especially when observing wildlife from a distance or at elevated positions. Each type caters to specific user preferences and viewing conditions.</p></p>
