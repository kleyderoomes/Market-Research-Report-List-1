<p><ul><li>Phenolic Ester Cold Box Binder (PECB)</li><li>Phenolic Urethane Cold Box Binder (PUCB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cold Box Casting Resin market primarily includes two types: Phenolic Ester Cold Box Binder (PECB) and Phenolic Urethane Cold Box Binder (PUCB). PECB is characterized by its excellent thermal properties and resistance to chemical degradation, making it ideal for complex metal castings. PUCB, on the other hand, offers superior flexibility and durability, enhancing the casting's mechanical properties. Both types are widely used in foundries to improve casting quality and reduce production times while maintaining environmentally friendly processes.</p></p>
