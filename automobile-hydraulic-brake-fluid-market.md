<p><p>The automobile hydraulic brake fluid market consists of different types categorized by their chemical composition and performance characteristics. DOT 3 brake fluids are glycol-based and suitable for normal driving conditions. DOT 4 fluids offer higher boiling points for more demanding applications. DOT 5 fluids are silicone-based, providing excellent moisture resistance but not compatible with other types. DOT 5.1 fluids, also glycol-based, combine high performance and compatibility, making them suitable for various vehicles, especially in extreme driving conditions.</p></p>
