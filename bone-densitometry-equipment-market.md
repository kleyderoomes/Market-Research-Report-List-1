<p><strong>The Bone Densitometry Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasound Bone Densitometer</li><li>X-ray Bone Densitometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone densitometry equipment market includes two main types: ultrasound bone densitometers and X-ray bone densitometers. Ultrasound bone densitometers utilize sound waves to assess bone density, offering a portable and radiation-free option, making them suitable for screenings and remote locations. In contrast, X-ray bone densitometers, often referred to as dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DEXA), provide highly accurate measurements and are widely used in clinical settings for diagnosing osteoporosis and assessing fracture risk based on bone mineral density.</p></p>
