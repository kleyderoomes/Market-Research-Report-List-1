<p><strong>The Rotating Disk Centrifugal Atomization Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Smooth Disc</li><li>Blade Disc</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotating disk centrifugal atomization equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: smooth disc and blade disc. Smooth disc atomizers utilize a flat, even surface to create a uniform mist of materials, ideal for producing fine particles with consistent size and shape. In contrast, blade disc atomizers feature angled blades that enhance turbulence, resulting in varying droplet sizes and improved material dispersion. Both types are crucial in diverse applications like coatings, pharmaceuticals, and advanced materials manufacturing.</p></p>
