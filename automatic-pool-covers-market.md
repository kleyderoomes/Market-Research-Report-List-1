<p><p>Automatic pool covers are categorized into two main types: hydraulic and electric. Hydraulic systems utilize fluid power to open and close the cover smoothly and efficiently, often offering better durability and reliability. Electric systems, on the other hand, rely on electrical motors to operate the cover, providing a user-friendly experience with quick deployment and retraction. Both types enhance pool safety, reduce evaporation, and maintain water temperature, making them popular choices among pool owners for convenience and energy efficiency.</p></p>
