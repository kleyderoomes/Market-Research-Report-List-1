<p><p>The Supply Chain Data Logger market is divided into two main types: disposable and reusable data loggers. Disposable data loggers are designed for single-use, providing a cost-effective solution for monitoring temperature, humidity, or other conditions during transport. These are often equipped with advanced features and connectivity options, making them suitable for ongoing supply chain management.</p></p>
