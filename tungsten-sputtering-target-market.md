<p><p>The Tungsten Sputtering Target market is primarily categorized into two types: Rotary Type and Non-Rotatable Type. Rotary Type targets are designed to rotate during the sputtering process, enhancing uniformity and efficiency in material deposition, making them suitable for larger substrates.
