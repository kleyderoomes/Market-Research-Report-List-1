<p><strong>The Single Dose Radiotherapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Beam Radiotherapy</li><li>Internal Beam Radiotherapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single Dose Radiotherapy market is primarily categorized into two types: External Beam Radiotherapy and Internal Beam Radiotherapy. External Beam Radiotherapy delivers high-energy radiation from outside the body to target tumors, ensuring precision while minimizing exposure to surrounding healthy tissues. In contrast, Internal Beam Radiotherapy, or brachytherapy, involves placing radioactive sources directly inside or near the tumor, allowing for localized treatment with less damage to adjacent normal tissue. Both methods aim to improve patient outcomes through effective cancer treatment strategies.</p></p>
