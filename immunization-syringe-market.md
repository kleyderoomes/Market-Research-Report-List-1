<p><p>The immunization syringe market is categorized into three main types: conventional syringes, safety syringes, and prefilled syringes. Conventional syringes are widely used for injections and are typically disposable. Safety syringes are designed with mechanisms to prevent needle stick injuries, enhancing safety for healthcare workers. Prefilled syringes contain a predetermined dose of medication, offering convenience and reducing preparation time. Each type plays a vital role in vaccination processes, improving efficiency, safety, and patient compliance in immunization practices.</p></p>
