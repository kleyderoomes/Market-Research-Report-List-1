<p><p>The polysulfide construction sealant market is divided into two main types: moisture cured and heat cured polysulfide sealants. Moisture cured polysulfide sealants react with atmospheric moisture to cure, making them suitable for various outdoor applications and providing excellent adhesion and flexibility. Heat cured polysulfide sealants, on the other hand, require elevated temperatures for curing, which enhances their mechanical properties and durability, often making them ideal for demanding industrial environments and applications like aerospace and automotive industries.</p></p>
