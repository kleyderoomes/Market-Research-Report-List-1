<p><p>Ethylene-Tetrafluoroethylene Copolymer (ETFE) market is segmented by polymerization methods: Suspension Polymerization, Mixed Phase Polymerization, and Emulsion Polymerization. </p><p>Suspension Polymerization involves dispersing the monomer in a liquid medium, resulting in high molecular weight products. </p><p>Mixed Phase Polymerization combines elements of both suspension and emulsion techniques, enhancing control over properties. </p><p>Emulsion Polymerization uses surfactants to stabilize the polymer in a liquid medium, producing fine emulsions and enabling desirable film and coating characteristics. These methods cater to diverse application needs in industries like construction, aerospace, and electrical.</p></p>
