<p><p>The Regional Anesthesia Disposables Market consists of three primary types: spinal, epidural, and peripheral. Spinal anesthesia involves injecting anesthetics into the subarachnoid space for lower body procedures. Epidural anesthesia places the anesthetic in the epidural space, often used for labor and surgeries, providing more control over pain relief. Peripheral anesthesia targets specific nerves to block sensation in localized areas, commonly employed in upper and lower limb surgeries. Each type utilizes specialized disposable equipment to ensure safety and efficacy.</p></p>
