<p><p>The lysimeter market features two primary types: weighing and non-weighing. Weighing lysimeters measure changes in soil moisture by tracking weight variations, providing precise data for hydrological studies and irrigation management. These are often used in research settings. Non-weighing lysimeters, on the other hand, rely on tanks or porous containers to collect drainage, offering simpler solutions for agricultural applications and environmental monitoring. Each type serves distinct needs, catering to various sectors from research institutions to agriculture.</p></p>
