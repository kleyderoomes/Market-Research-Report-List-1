<p><ul><li>Horizontal Closed Tank</li><li>Silo/ Vertical Closed Tank</li><li>Open Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The milk cooling tank market comprises three primary types: horizontal closed tanks, silo/vertical closed tanks, and open tanks. Horizontal closed tanks are designed for efficient cooling and occupy less floor space, making them suitable for smaller operations. Silo or vertical closed tanks, offering a larger capacity, facilitate bulk storage and better cooling efficiency, ideal for dairy farms with high milk production. Open tanks allow for easier access and maintenance but may be less effective in maintaining optimal temperatures, often used for small-scale operations.</p></p>
