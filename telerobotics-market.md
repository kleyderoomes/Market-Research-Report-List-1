<p><ul><li>Wireless Network Connections</li><li>Tethered Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telerobotics market is primarily divided into two types based on connection methods: wireless network connections and tethered connections. Wireless network connections rely on unbounded communication, enabling remote operation without physical constraints, facilitating mobility and flexibility. This type often employs technologies like Wi-Fi, 4G, and 5G. In contrast, tethered connections involve physical cables linking the robot to the control system, offering stable, high-bandwidth communication suitable for critical tasks where reliability is paramount, such as in medical or industrial applications.</p></p>
