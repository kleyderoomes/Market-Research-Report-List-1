<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Hollow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Olefin Fiber market is typically categorized into solid and hollow types. Solid olefin fibers offer excellent strength and durability, making them ideal for applications like upholstery and automotive interiors. In contrast, hollow olefin fibers are lightweight and possess enhanced insulation properties, making them suitable for thermal protection and outdoor gear. Both types are favored for their moisture resistance and easy maintenance, contributing to their popularity across various industries, including textiles, construction, and automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
