<p><p>The plastics processing machinery market comprises several key types. Injection molding machines (IMM) are used to produce precise, complex shapes by injecting molten plastic into molds. Extrusion machines continuously shape plastic by forcing it through a die, producing items like pipes and sheets. Blow molding machines (BMM) create hollow objects by inflating heated plastic in a mold. Other machinery includes techniques such as rotational molding and thermoforming, catering to diverse applications in packaging, automotive, and consumer products.</p></p>
